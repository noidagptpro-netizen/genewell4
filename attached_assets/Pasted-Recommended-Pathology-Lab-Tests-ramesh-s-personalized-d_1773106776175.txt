Moving from guesswork to data-driven health decisions requires clinical testing. These tests are recommended specifically for
YOU based on your quiz responses, health scores, BMI, medical conditions, and wellness goals. All tests are standard pathology
panels available at major Indian diagnostic centers (Thyrocare, Dr. Lal PathLabs, SRL Diagnostics, Metropolis).
Priority Tests (Based on Your Profile)
• Complete Blood Count (CBC) — Baseline health marker. Cost: Rs.200-400.
• Fasting Blood Glucose (FBS) — Screens for pre-diabetes and diabetes. Cost: Rs.80-150.
• Vitamin D (25-hydroxyvitamin D) — 76% of urban Indians are deficient. Cost: Rs.600-1,200.
• Thyroid Profile (T3, T4, TSH, Anti-TPO) — Critical for metabolic regulation and fatigue assessment. Cost: Rs.400-800.
• Vitamin B12 & Folate — Foundational for nervous system health. Cost: Rs.500-900.
• HbA1c & Fasting Insulin — 3-month blood sugar average and insulin resistance. Cost: Rs.300-600.
• Lipid Profile — Cardiovascular risk assessment. Cost: Rs.200-500.
Advanced Biomarkers (Premium Tier)
• Advanced Lipid Profile with ApoB — More accurate cardiovascular predictor. Cost: Rs.800-1,500.
• hs-CRP (High-Sensitivity C-Reactive Protein) — Measures systemic inflammation. Cost: Rs.300-600.
• Liver Function Tests (SGOT, SGPT, ALP, GGT) — Detects fatty liver. Cost: Rs.300-500.
• Kidney Function Tests (Creatinine, BUN, Uric Acid) — Baseline organ health. Cost: Rs.400-700.
• Magnesium (Serum) — Critical for 300+ enzymatic reactions. Cost: Rs.200-400.
Testing Schedule
• Baseline: Get all recommended tests done BEFORE starting your wellness plan (Week 0)
• Follow-up: Retest at 12 weeks (90 days) to measure the impact of your lifestyle changes
• Maintenance: Annual testing for ongoing health monitoring
